Bag Management Guidelines
  • Cabinet: The default Cabinet bag cannot be deleted or renamed - it serves as the central storage
  • Naming: Use descriptive names like "Emergency Bag 1", "ICU Mobile Cart", "Trauma Kit A"
  • Deletion: Bags can only be deleted if they contain no items - transfer items first
  • Organization: Consider creating bags based on location, medical specialty, or usage frequency
  • Tracking: Each bag maintains its own inventory that can be viewed and managed separately
